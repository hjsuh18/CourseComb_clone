At Princeton, your class schedule can really make or break your semester. Students currently spend countless hours on course selection websites manually trying out different schedules to get their optimal schedule. We propose a simple solution to course selection, which automates the process of generating students' course schedules. Users are only required to input their courses of interest and preferences and the website's algorithm takes care of the rest. Use CourseComb to find your favorite schedule in just a few clicks.

https://coursecomb.herokuapp.com/
